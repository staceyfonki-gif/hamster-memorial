In Loving Memory of My Hamster Nathaniel

This website is a tribute to my loving Nathaniel, Nathie, who was a loving companion and a part of our daily lives for 2 years and 7 months. He was loved and cared for by his both mommies.

His Story

We got Nathie when he was three months old, from a pet shop in Duisburg, Germany. He was very shy and introverted at the beginning and never wanted to be seen or touched by anybody. When he got older, we gained mutual trust, but his personality was still shy and introverted. He was very curious, loved having new toys and things to chew. He brought us so much joy and comfort.
